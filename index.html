I am a Senior Data Scientist at the Intelligent Clinical Care Center (IC3) at the University of Florida, specializing in AI in healthcare, AI in education, and human-computer interaction. My research centers on developing real-time intelligent AI systems to support medical diagnosis, treatment, and education, with a strong emphasis on safety, ethics, and the implementation of advanced, transparent, and fair AI algorithms. In addition to my research, I am also a passionate educator teaching biomedical professionals on cutting-edge AI applications in medicine.
